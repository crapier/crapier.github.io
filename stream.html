RTMP Streams
Has a lower delay of about 1-5 seconds, but requires flash player.
HLS Streams
Has a higher delay of about 20-40 seconds, but doesn't require flash. Allows for around 5 minutes rewind and seeking of current stream.
VLC URLs
Copy RTMP or HLS url into network stream for VLC or other media player that allows network stream. Will have associated delays as mentioned above.
